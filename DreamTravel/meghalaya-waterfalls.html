Meghalaya Waterfalls - The Land of Waterfalls

Meghalaya, known as the "Abode of Clouds," is home to some of the most beautiful waterfalls in India...

Best Time to Visit:

The best time to visit is from October to April, when the weather is perfect for exploring the waterfalls.

Top Attractions Nearby:

  • Shillong Peak: The highest point in Shillong, offering panoramic views of the city.
  • Nohkalikai Falls: One of the tallest waterfalls in India.
  • Elephant Falls: A famous waterfall located near Shillong.

Visitor Tips:

  • Carry a raincoat or umbrella as showers are frequent in Meghalaya.
  • Wear good trekking shoes if you plan to hike around the waterfalls.
