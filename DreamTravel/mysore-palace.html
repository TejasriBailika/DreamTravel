Mysore Palace - The Jewel of Karnataka

Mysore Palace, also known as Amba Vilas Palace, is a stunning historical palace and a symbol of Karnataka’s rich heritage...

Best Time to Visit:

Visit during the Dussehra festival in October to witness grand celebrations and illuminated palace views.

Top Attractions Nearby:

  • Chamundi Hill: A temple dedicated to Goddess Chamundeshwari.
  • Brindavan Gardens: Famous for its musical fountain shows.

Visitor Tips:

  • Visit in the evening to see the palace lit up with thousands of lights.
