Qutub Minar - A Tower of Victory

Qutub Minar is a UNESCO World Heritage Site located in Mehrauli, Delhi. It is the tallest brick minaret in the world...

Best Time to Visit:

The best time to visit Qutub Minar is during the winter months, between October and March...

Top Attractions Nearby:

  • Alai Minar: Another unfinished minaret located near Qutub Minar.
  • Iron Pillar: A 7-meter tall iron pillar from ancient India that has remained rust-free for over 2,000 years.
  • Mehrauli Archaeological Park: A park that features the ruins of several historical structures.

Popular Activities:

  • Climbing to the top of Qutub Minar to get a panoramic view of the surroundings (if open).
  • Exploring the surrounding archaeological ruins and learning about their history.
  • Photography, especially during golden hour when the sunlight casts a beautiful glow on the Minar.

Visitor Tips:

  • Visit early in the morning to avoid large crowds.
  • Wear comfortable shoes as the area involves walking through ruins.
  • Respect the historical significance of the site and avoid damaging any of the ancient structures.
